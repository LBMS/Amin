Awareness Programe
  1. 1981- Arranging seminars in the World Disabled Year, developing people's awareness.
  2. 1982- Setting up Training and Education Centre for the Handicapped Rehabilitation Up till now 1000 handicapped persons established.
  3. 1983- Having a remarkable contribution towards inducing the blind to join Louis Braille Memorial School for the Blind, he serves at present then by dint of his sincerest as well as dauntless efforts the school.
  4. 1990- Organizing an All India Athletic Meet for all sorts of Handicapped. Participation of 1285 handicapped from 17 states in 48 discipline and setting up vocational training center and and its management. Instituting and management of the Vocational Training Center for making the self-reliance programme a success.
  5. 1991- Extending cooperation to setting up Special Institution for the Mentally Retarded
  6. 2000- Organizing an All India Chess Tournament for the blind in collaboration with NIVH, Dehradun.
  7. 2001- Extending help to the flood victims, setting up medical camp, providing education to the commoners about the handicapped children.
  8. 2005- Taking serious efforts in setting up a Special School for the Blind in the Nadia District
  9. 2006- Setting up social Rehabilitation Camp through the efforts of the Rajya Protibandhi Sammilani.
  10. 2007- Setting up a Primary School for the Handicapped as well as non- handicapped Children at Baksha
  11. 2008- Imparting education and training apart, making provisions for Environmental Training through community- Based Rehabilitation.
  12. 2010- Taking part in the workshop for the Eastern Region Handicapped, organized by the RCI, Jharkhand, thereby showing the beacon to all the participants.
  13. 2010- Organizing an All India Chess Competition for the Blind at Kolkata.
  14. 2011- Organizing Indentification Camp and Distributing Aids and Appliances at remote villages.
  15. 2011- Participating in the International Seminar and acting as a delegate of the Handicapped people
  16. 2012- Alongside education and training, taking up multifarious programmes including Handicap Identification Camp, Free medical Camp, Pulse Polio Camp & Thalassemia Identification.
  17. 2013- Organizing Awareness programmes in collaboration with NIVH KOLKATA.
  • VOCATIONAL TRAINING COURSES:-
    Those students who have no aptitude for higher studies are being admitted in the following job oriented Vocational Training Courses in this institution :-

    1. Computer Training Course : After completion of their Secondary or Graduation, the students are admitted in this course.This training helps our students for finding respectable jobs after they pass out from this institute. This Department has trained more than 50 students.
    2. Vocational Training:- Vocational Training is a part and parcel of the curriculum for any handicapped person. The programme which starts in a merge way by starting making of paper bags has gradually added production of Incense sticks, Chalk pencils, Candles and many useful and attractive articles from Bamboo, Hemp/Coir, Cane etc. Few aged handicapped person have been trained rehabilitated by Gramophone Company Ltd.(HMV). A production centre for spices Powder has been started and seven handicapped persons are earning their livelihood from this centre. Money earned out of different products of the V.T. centre is spent on maintenance of the people engaged.
